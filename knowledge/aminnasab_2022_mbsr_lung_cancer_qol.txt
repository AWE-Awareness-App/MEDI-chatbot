Title: Aminnasab 2022 – MBSR for Lung Cancer (Randomized Study)

Type: Quasi-experimental clinical trial
Population: Lung cancer patients
Intervention: 8-week MBSR
Year: 2022

Topic Tags:
- lung_cancer
- cancer
- quality_of_life
- stress
- mindfulness
- rct

----------------------------------------

OVERVIEW

This clinical study evaluated the effectiveness of MBSR therapy on quality of life in lung cancer patients.

----------------------------------------

KEY FINDINGS

Patients who received MBSR showed:

✔ Significant improvement in overall quality of life
✔ Improved mental health
✔ Reduced stress
✔ Improved emotional functioning

Improvements were statistically significant compared to control group.

----------------------------------------

INTERPRETATION

Mindfulness programs may support emotional wellbeing and coping in individuals undergoing cancer treatment.

----------------------------------------

PROGRAM STRUCTURE

- 8-week intervention
- Weekly group sessions
- Breathing meditation
- Body awareness
- Gentle yoga

----------------------------------------

MEDI-SAFE LANGUAGE

• “Research suggests mindfulness therapy can help people with lung cancer cope emotionally.”
• “It may improve quality of life during treatment.”

----------------------------------------

IMPORTANT BOUNDARY

Mindfulness supports emotional health but does not treat cancer.

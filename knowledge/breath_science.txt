----------------------------------------

OVERVIEW

Breathing directly influences the autonomic nervous system.

Slow, rhythmic breathing:
- Increases vagal tone
- Reduces heart rate
- Improves emotional regulation

----------------------------------------

KEY MECHANISM

Respiratory Sinus Arrhythmia (RSA):
Heart rate naturally increases on inhale and decreases on exhale.

Longer exhale activates parasympathetic pathways.

----------------------------------------

EVIDENCE

Slow breathing (around 5–6 breaths per minute):
- Reduces anxiety
- Improves HRV
- Enhances emotional control

----------------------------------------

MEDI-SAFE LANGUAGE

• “Lengthening your exhale signals safety to your body.”
• “Breathing slowly helps your nervous system settle.”

Title: Sleep Science – Sleep Regulation and Mental Health

Type: Sleep physiology review

Topic Tags:
- sleep
- circadian_rhythm
- stress
- nervous_system
- mental_health

----------------------------------------

OVERVIEW

Sleep is regulated by:
- Circadian rhythm (internal clock)
- Sleep pressure (adenosine accumulation)
- Nervous system state

Stress disrupts sleep by increasing sympathetic activation.

----------------------------------------

KEY INSIGHTS

- Poor sleep increases anxiety and emotional reactivity.
- Chronic stress reduces sleep quality.
- Parasympathetic activation supports deeper sleep.
- Breath regulation can improve sleep onset.

----------------------------------------

CONNECTION TO MEDITATION

Mindfulness practices:
- Reduce rumination
- Lower physiological arousal
- Improve sleep latency
- Increase sleep quality

----------------------------------------

MEDI-SAFE LANGUAGE

• “Calming your nervous system can improve sleep.”
• “Breathing slowly before bed can help your body transition into rest.”
